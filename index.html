3–4L water daily. प्रत्येक 30 min training नंतर 500ml घ्या. Dehydration = no gains.

SLEEP = GAINS
7–9 hours mandatory. Growth hormone रात्री release होतो. झोपेत muscle build होतो.

PROGRESSIVE OVERLOAD
